Who are we?

We're a small store opening up shortly dedicated to being a celebration of all things horror, whether old or new!

At our store, you'll find plenty of horror-related merchandise and the classics that you're unable to find anywhere else!

Why?

Simple; we're nostalgic for the classics!

Our store owner and most of the staff are fossils who are simply unable to let go of the past, and horror happens to be our favourite genre, so here we are!

We've grown tired of the usual comicbook movies with quips and PG rated violence. Come join us in our reverence of the past, where it was rated M and characters actually died!
